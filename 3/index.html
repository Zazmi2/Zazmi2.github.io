A.4: Blend Images into a Mosaic

The images were blended into mosaics using weighted averaging to reduce edge artifacts. Here are three mosaics with their corresponding source images. The main pipeline included, warping one of the images into the other image's plane. I also added a translation component into the Homography matrix to account for the offset from the warping> I then blended them together and placed it into a mosaic.
